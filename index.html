Hey There

We know managing money isn't easy. Most Americans don't have enough put away for an emergency, let alone a vacation. At Digit, we're trying to change that. We think the right technology can make managing personal finances a little easier for everyone.

We're working to improve people's financial health, so they can focus on the fun stuff. You should be able to prepare for surprises, needs, and dreams without changing the way you live.

Our team is in this for the journey. If you think these problems are worth solving, then we hope you'll join us.

    Introduction

    Having fun while working is a good thing to me. I love to look good, feel good and make others feel good about them self. I live where I work because that is the family I will be with all the time. 

    I have traveled to four continents and different countries. Embracing others culture and listening to diversified view to solving a problem is the best thing I learn on my adventures.

    A Full-stack engineer with a banking background. I served the business world for five years and discovered that helping clients solve personal financial problems was a tremendous responsibility. I built several strong relationships that lasted outside work because I treated every client personally with respect.

    My entrepreneurship spirit leads me to quit my job I love to create a plan for my long-time dream, having my own business. Going through a non-traditional school to become a full-stack engineer. I will build an international trade in the future. Working with and serving other engineers in the next 7 - 10 years will solidify my expertise and let me contribute the best I can to the development of other projects to make them succeed.
